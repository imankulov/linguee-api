esgotar (algo) verb—

exhaust (sth.) v (exhausted, exhausted)

Atividades sustentáveis não esgotam os recursos naturais. — Sustainable activities do not exhaust the natural resources.

deplete (sth.) v (depleted, depleted)

O cultivo intensivo esgota o nitrogênio do solo. — Intensive farming depletes the soil of nitrogen.

sell out v (sold, sold)

Os ingressos para a final vão esgotar muito rápido. — The tickets for the final game will sell out very quickly.

drain v (drained, drained)

Trabalhar por mais de oito horas esgota minha energia. — Working for more than eight hours drains my energy.

tax v (taxed, taxed)

A longa espera no aeroporto esgotou a minha paciência. — The long wait at the airport taxed my patience.
